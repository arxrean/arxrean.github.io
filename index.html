Ziyi Kou

Senior Research Scientist - Meta Reality Lab Research

I am a senior research scientist at Meta Reality Lab. My research focuses on visual-language-action (VLA) modeling, multimodal representation learning and 3D pose tracking. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) department at University of Notre Dame. My advisor is Dr. Xiangliang Zhang. I received my master's degree of Computer Science from University of Rochester and my bachelor's degree of Software Engineering from Chongqing University.
